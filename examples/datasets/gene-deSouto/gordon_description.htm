Description:

 

 The pathological distinction between malignant pleural mesothelioma (MPM) and adenocarcinoma (AD) of the lung can be cumbersome using established methods. The authors propose that a simple technique, based on the expression levels of a small number of genes, can be useful in the early and accurate diagnosis of MPM and lung cancer. This method is designed to accurately distinguish between genetically disparate tissues using gene expression ratios and rationally chosen thresholds.
